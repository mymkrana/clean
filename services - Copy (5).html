PROFESSIONAL ONE-OFF CLEANS IN AND AROUND MELBOURNE

Whether it’s a small 2-3 hour clean you're after or it’s a spring clean of your entire Melbourne home, we can do it all! We understand there are a variety of different reasons why you may need to book a one-off clean and we have the cleaners to be able to help out.

SMALL CLEANS

If you are looking for a quick clean of certain areas of your house, then our “Hourly Rate” service would be best for you. On our hourly rates we can be completely flexible with the cleaning we provide and simply do as much work as possible in the time booked.

SPECIFIC AREAS

These types of one off cleans are great for if you need certain specific areas cleaned (e.g. kitchen, bathrooms, floors) or if you just are looking for a quick spruce up of certain areas.

Book Now

LARGER CLEANS / WHOLE HOUSE CLEAN

If you are looking for a one off clean for your whole house, our “Flat Rate Service” is probably the way to go. We offer flat rates which cover all the general cleaning in your home without a fixed time limit. For more information on these services, please see the below links.

Book Now

DEEP CLEANS

If your house has not been cleaned for more than around 1-2 months then we strongly recommend booking our “Flat Rate Service” with the “Spring Clean” extra. This upgrades your flat rate to allow for the extra work required to really bring your house up to our standards.

SPRING CLEAN

The key difference between our flat rates and our spring cleans is the condition of your home. If your house has gathered a degree of dust/dirt/grime over a period of approx. 1-2+ months, then considerably more time and work will be needed to bring the house up to our standards. The “Spring Clean” extra allows for this extra work.
