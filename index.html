WE HAVE REVOLUTIONISED HOUSE CLEANING

When Maid to Clean was founded there was no easy way to book a cleaning service online – you needed to call a company, organise a quote and then book a service days later. We have changed the way cleaning services are booked through offering a convenient online booking platform. On top of this Maid to Clean takes pride in attracting and keeping the best cleaners through our efficient systems and genuine care for our cleaners and customers. We also understand that the secret to a great cleaning service is finding the perfect match of cleaning team and customer. We carefully hand pick every one of our cleaners and do everything we can to make sure they are capable of providing the excellent service we strive for. In general, we only look for cleaners who have been cleaning for 2-3+ years, are extremely professional, but also fun and friendly at the same time.
